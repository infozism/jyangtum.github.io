Bio

I finished my doctoral study with Magna cum laude at Lehrstuhl für Kartographie, Technische Universtität München with topic, Labeling Spatial Trajectories in Road Networks using Probabilistic Graphical Models supervised by Prof. Dr.-Ing Liqiu Meng. I graduated from National University of Defense Technology, China in 2010 with a B.Eng. and M.Eng in Information Engineering.

Research Interests

Apply Machine Learning to solve geospatial problems. In particular, I work on graphcial model, spatial trajecotry data.
